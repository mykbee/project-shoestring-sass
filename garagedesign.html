220 East 8th Street Garage

Austin, TX
Location

The 220 East 8th Street Garage is located at the intersection of 8th and San Jacinto Streets directly across the street from the Omni Hotel. The Social Security and Federal buildings are across the street from the garage.

Access and Security

The garage has an access card that allows you to come and go as needed. Parking spaces can be accessed 24 hours a day and 7 days a week. This means that if you work late your space is still guaranteed. The month to month contract covers unrestricted use.

The card reader and gate arm ensure access is available to only those with a valid parking card. Using state-of-the-art technology (card reader and a computer database), we can determine who is in the garage at any time.

Stairwell exit doors are secured with combination pushpin locks. The locks are designed to keep non-tenants out of the garage. Please keep all doors closed and please don't give out the combination.

Pricing

We are a contract-only garage, and lease to individuals and companies who need space. We offer both month-to-month and term agreements up to 12 months. The rent is $130.00 per space plus applicable city of Austin sales taxes. The parking spaces are first-come, first-serve.

In addition to the unreserved spaces there are a limited number of reserved spaces available. The reserved spaces lease for $200.00 per month plus applicable taxes. Spaces are only reserved from 6:00am to 6:00pm, Monday through Friday.

Please contact us if you have any questions or need help with the lease agreement. Downloadable forms are available by clicking the link at the top of the page.
